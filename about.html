Hey There!

Since you asked...

Here’s a little bit about myself.
Born in Northern Japan. Raised in the Great White North known as Canada with European background. Traveled the world as a young boy and always aspired to be an artist with my mother being a Designer.
This allowed me to see the world from so many different perspectives, which I used as inspiration to start photography. I worked as a photographer for 1 year at a Digital Marketing Company doing mostly product photography, but as time grew I started to explore different styles such as portrait and landscape and even some wildlife photography.
I try to be as flexible as possible so if you’re looking to collaborate with me, please don’t hesitate to contact me.

Have a great day!
