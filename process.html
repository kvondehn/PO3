Process

After we have booked your appointment, which can be done on the contact page. We will meet at the designated spot and start right away.

100s of photos will be taken to try and get the best shots for you and your liking.

Once we have the photos taken,
I will go home and edit and process the best shots on Adobe Lightroom and Photoshop.
I will then send them to you with watermarking so you can see which photos you would like to purchase.

Once you have picked out your favourites, I can adjust the photos to your liking as shown below if you have any requests on how the photo will look!

Once that is complete, I will send you a list of the photos that we have worked on and you can choose to either purchase the whole set or just a few photos.

Each photo will be around $30.
The whole set will be around 10-15 photos, and will be $150.

If you need more photos or have a special request we will determine the cost during the planning phase.

Once you have picked out your favourites and sent payment, I will send a link with the jpeg files for you to download! I can provide files optimized for publishing online, or for printing so just let me know what you need and I can cater to your needs!

Thank You!
