Hi There!

Here's a little something about myself!
I was born in Northern Japan. Raised in the Great White North known as Canada and I also have European background.
Traveled the world as a young boy and always aspired to be an artist like my mother.
Traveling the world allowed me to see the world from so many different perspectives, which inspired me to start photography.

I worked as a photographer for 1 year at a Digital Marketing Company doing mostly product photography, but as time grew I started to explore different styles such as portrait and landscape photography and even some wildlife photography.
I try to be as flexible as possible so if you’re looking to collaborate with me, please don’t hesitate to contact me.

Have a great day!
